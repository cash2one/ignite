FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions, we have answers.

Why is the game called IGNITE?

We are igniting torches, and sharing the light around the kingdom. Taking the light and passing it on.

How did IGNITE come about?

After a long day of playing Ingress (an augmented reality video game), Lana and Amelia talked about how awesome it would be if a big group from school played.

What do I need to play?

An electronic device with a camera that is able to access the internet. A camera is needed to read the QR codes on the torches or posters around the school; this is how the points are generated. For any handheld devices such as iPads, iPhones, Samsung Galaxy Tablets and the like, a QR code reader can be downloaded and installed off the device’s respective app store for free. For other devices such as Chromebooks, laptops and desktop computers, a QR code reader can be accessed on the internet. For example here ( http://webqr.com ).

How do I play?

Go and have a look at the Gameplay pages for more information.

What is a QR code?

A QR code (quick response code) is a type of 2D bar code that is used to provide quick and easy access to information through a smartphone or smart device.

What is a QR code reader?

A QR code reader is an application that reads QR codes through the use of a webcam or camera attached or a part of a smart device.

May I change my username? Pretty please with a fairy on top?

No.

How do I gain points for my house?

To gain points for your house, you must scan a QR code obtained from either a poster, torch or online. If you scan a QR code which has an identifier the same colour as your house e.g. a jelicoe member scans a green torch, then you will earn two points for your house. If you scan a QR code which has either an identifier with a different colour to your house or white, then you will earn one point for your house.

Why did I only get one point for scanning a poster?

Posters that have no house colours on them are worth only one point, they are general points.

How many torches and posters are available to be claimed?

Check out the torch registry. We cannot guarantee that the posters and torches originally set up will be the same number of ones available as they are subjected to the wild.

What do I win?

May I ask, what is better than the feeling of dominating the world? This is sort of like that, except on a much smaller scale… no guarantees. Your points go towards house points.
